AI Capabilities

Every service is an AI agent

We didn't bolt AI onto an existing cable business. We built an AI-native platform that happens to produce physical cables as an output artefact.

Agent: LengthOptimizer™

Intelligent Custom Lengths

Transformer-based length prediction from natural-language floorplan descriptions. Specify in metres or just describe the room — the model handles the rest.

Agent: ChromaVLAN™

Semantic Colour Intelligence

CableGPT-4 learns your network schema and generates an optimal colour schedule, cross-referenced against IEEE and ANSI/TIA standards via RAG pipeline.

Agent: PatchMind™

Agentic Panel Termination

Our autonomous termination agent ingests your floorplan as a vector embedding and outputs a labelled patch schedule with zero human intervention required.

Agent: CatBoost™ (the cable kind)

AI-Matched Cable Category

Gradient-boosted classification model evaluates your switch PoE budget, NAS throughput, and future 10 GbE aspirations to recommend Cat6A vs Cat8 with confidence scores.

Agent: NeuralFluke™

Neural Continuity Certification

AI-interpreted TDR waveform analysis signs every cable with a cryptographically-verified continuity certificate. No cable ships without a model-confirmed pass.

Agent: Topology Copilot™

Generative Network Design

Share your rack diagram via drag-and-drop. Our multimodal vision model reads it and generates a complete cable schedule, BOM, and rack elevation in seconds.

Agentic Workflow

From natural language to doorstep

Four autonomous agents orchestrate your order. You only need to describe what you want.

Step 01 — Ingest

Natural Language Intake

Type what you need. CableGPT-4 extracts structured intent from your message via a fine-tuned extraction head.

Step 02 — Reason

Topology Inference

LengthOptimizer and ChromaVLAN agents run in parallel, each contributing to a unified cable schedule with uncertainty estimates.

Step 03 — Build

Human-in-the-Loop Fabrication

AI-generated spec is handed to a human craftsperson (MT). NeuralFluke validates every cable before it's packaged.

Step 04 — Deliver

Certified Physical Output

Cables arrive with an AI-signed certificate, QR-linked test report, and a prompt to fine-tune CableGPT-4 on your topology.
